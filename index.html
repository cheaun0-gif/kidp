Studio Hyeong

hyeong is a design studio formed by two college students who are active in Korea. It pursues user-friendly design based on ergonomics and molding, and presents witty objects that naturally penetrate into everyday life. In addition, I would like to explore the unanswered area, design future values, and explore various possibilities to make a positive impact on society.
